Pairwise θπ in het-inversion vs collinear segments
Scatter of mean pairwise θπ between sample pairs, computed separately in heterozygous-inversion segments (y-axis) and collinear background (x-axis). Each dot = one sample pair; highlight cross-K-cluster pairs (e.g. K1×K7). Dashed identity = expectation under no inversion-specific divergence. Pairs above the identity line carry inversion- specific haplotype divergence — direct mechanistic counterpart to Fig. S22 of the Massilani et al. archaic- introgression paper (DXY in Neanderthal vs Denisova segments).
