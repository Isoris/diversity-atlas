S5 · The all226 → pruned81 collapse
central finding

F_ROH × ancestry differs significantly in the full 226-sample panel (KW P = 2.9 × 10⁻⁴) but the signal collapses to non-significance in pruned81 (P = 0.59). H × ancestry is non-significant in either panel. Interpretation: covariance reflects recent shared inbreeding among siblings within ancestry components, not divergence among founder lineages.
